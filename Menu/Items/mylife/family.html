My family

   Being born in an Albanian family means you are lucky. In my family there are my parents, my sister, my nephew and my cat, Defne. We always support and protect each other in everything. Albanians in general have a sense of humor so when we gather together with other relatives too we spend so wonderful time. Also Albanian parents take the role of a parent but also the role of a friend to their children too when needed.
   My dad's name is Sotir and he was a football player for the National Team and for the "Besa" team. His position was a goalkeeper. After he retired he become the coach of the "Besa" team. Since I was little I used to go to the football field and watch the matches. I can say that I was raised in the football field. Maybe that's why I love sports this much.
   My mom's name is Engjellushe and she has finished Economics and owns her own clothing shop. She is really hardworking too. As a mother she used to be really strict with me and my sister, especially for the school grades.
   My sister's name is Gloria and she is the Assistand Editor in Insight Turkey and also she is a Researcher in the Energy Studies. She couninued her studies for Master Degree in Turkey. For 7 years she lives in Turkey now and is married and have a son. My sister is 10 years older than me.
   I have a little wonderful nephew called Eymen. He is going to turn 2 years old now and he is really cute and energetic.
   Not to forget the little princess that we have and she is Defne. She is our cat and she is 4 years old now.
   I am so happy that I have a family like this.
