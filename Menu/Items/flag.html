The Flag of Albania is a red flag with a silhouetted black double-headed eagle in the center. The red stands for bravery, strength, valour and bloodshed, while the double-headed eagle represents the sovereign state of Albania. Another meaning for the double-heades black eagle is that the eagle sees and protects all the Albanians and the Albanian territories that lie beyond Albanian borders. The flag was established as the national flag of Albania when the country gained its independence from the Ottoman empire in 1912.
